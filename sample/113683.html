Bab Ayur (Persian: باب ايور‎‎, also Romanized as Bāb Ayūr; also known as ‘Ayūr, Bāba Aiyūr, Bābā Alīvar, Bābā Ayūr, Bābā Ayyūr, and Bovāyūr)[1] is a village in Siyakh Darengun Rural District, in the Central District of Shiraz County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 371, in 85 families.[2]
